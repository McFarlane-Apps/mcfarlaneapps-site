KitchenSync

Dinner, synchronised. Set the time you want to eat — KitchenSync works backwards.

iOS Android Coming soon

Getting a roast on the table — everything ready at the same time — takes a lot of mental overhead. KitchenSync removes it. Tell the app when you want to eat, add your dishes and how long each takes, and it produces a precise schedule: what to start, and exactly when.

During cooking, the app tracks what's active, surfaces what's up next, and fires alarms at the right moment so you can stay out of the kitchen and focus on everything else.
