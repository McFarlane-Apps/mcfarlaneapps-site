YuzuSSH

SSH from your pocket. A lightweight, beautiful SSH client for Android and iOS.

Android iOS
Visit site ↗

YuzuSSH is a full SSH client that fits in your hand. Save your connections once, tap to open a session, and get a proper xterm-compatible terminal with 256-colour support — whether you're on the sofa or on a flight.

Built around touch, not ported from desktop. A dedicated mobile keyboard overlay adds ESC, TAB, CTRL, ALT, arrow keys, and sticky modifiers — so you can actually get work done, not just read logs. Password and key authentication, with secure credential storage.

Your data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking — credentials and saved connections never leave your phone. Built with Flutter for native performance on both platforms.
