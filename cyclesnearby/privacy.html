External companies assist with service delivery and analysis. These providers access personal information but cannot disclose or misuse it.

The service contains third-party links. External sites operate independently; the developer assumes no responsibility for their policies.

Children's Privacy

The service doesn't intentionally collect information from users under thirteen. Any discovered instances are deleted immediately.
