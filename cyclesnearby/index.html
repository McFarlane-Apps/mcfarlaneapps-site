CyclesNearby

Quickly find your closest available Santander bikes, or empty docks, with ease.

Android
Visit site ↗

CyclesNearby is a no-nonsense Android app for London's Santander Cycles network. Open the app and you immediately see the closest docks, how many bikes are available, and how many empty spaces are waiting — ranked by distance from wherever you are.

Built for the two moments that matter: finding a bike when you need one, and finding a dock when you're trying to end a journey. No accounts, no tracking, no clutter — just live station data, cleanly presented.
